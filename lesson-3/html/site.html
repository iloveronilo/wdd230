Enjoy Mariveles


Site Purpose :

Hi! There everyone! my name is Ronilo. I am a genuine residence of Mariveles, Bataan in the beautiful country of the Philippines. I am a traveler. My purpose of this website is to Promote tourism here in the place that I lived in so that everyone who likes to visit the Philippines would be acknowledged.

To reveal Mariveles Town's hidden beauty, to increase tourism in the area, and to reveal its genuine attractiveness and charm to the people. After seeing a few tourist attractions, you will be looking for a way to have a good time while remaining relaxed.

Mariveles is situated around Mariveles Bay, a wide bay on the Bataan Peninsula's southern edge. It is bordered on the east by Manila Bay and on the west by the West Philippine Sea. Mariveles Naval Section Base, constructed for the United States Asiatic Fleet on 22 July 1941 and surrendered to the Imperial Japanese Army on 9 April 1942, was located in Mariveles Bay.
