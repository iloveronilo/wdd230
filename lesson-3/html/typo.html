Typography :

I utilized the following type fonts to draw the viewer's attention to the website, which adds originality and improves the user experience quality.

In its most basic form, typography is the skill of arranging letters and words in such a manner that the material is readable, clear, and visually appealing to the reader. Typography is the study of font style, look, and structure with the goal of eliciting certain emotions and communicating specific messages.


Font Family Use

  • Sub-title : "Cinzel",serif, Tahoma, sans-serif
  • Paragraph, list, etc. : "Yuji Syuku", serif, Georgia, monospace
